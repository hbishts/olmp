RD Services Token (S)

Registered Device Service (RDS) enables customers and end users to register their fingerprint devices with UIDAI. This service is required in order for the Aadhaar Authentication server to uniquely identify and validate the devices to perform any Aadhaar based authentication(as per the UIDAI guidelines).
