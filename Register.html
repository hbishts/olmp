(j) Public Devices are biometric capture devices that provides Aadhaar-compliant biometric data to the application, which in turn encrypts the data before using it for authentication purposes

(k) Purchase Order means a commercial document offer issued by the Purchaser to the Device Provider, indicating types, quantities, and agreed prices for Registered Devices in (L0/ L1 configuation) and associated Registered Devices Services, delivered for the devices.
